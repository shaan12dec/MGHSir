Our Research

Prof. MGH Zaidi's research focuses on Polymer Science & Technology, exploring advanced polymerization and modification techniques in supercritical fluids to enhance material properties. His work extends to solid waste management, aiming for sustainable solutions in construction materials and multifunctional coatings. Additionally, he is actively involved in Green Nanotechnology, developing eco-friendly nanomaterials for various applications, emphasizing sustainability and innovation in material science. Prof. Zaidi present research can be summarised in these categories:

  • Polymer Science & Technology
  • Polymerization and polymer modification in supercritical fluids
  • Solid waste management, Sustainable Construction Materials
  • Multifunctional coatings, Green Nanotechnology
